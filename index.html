How to play

Three rules. That's it.

1

Watch the lanes

Beats fall down four color-coded lanes. Each lane maps to a key — D, F, J, or K — or just tap the lane directly.

2

Hit the line

When a beat reaches the bright line at the bottom, press its key. The closer to perfect, the more points you score.

3

Keep the combo

Every hit grows your combo multiplier. Miss three beats and the level ends — but you can always jump back in.
